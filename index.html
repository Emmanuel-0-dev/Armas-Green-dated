Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our fresh greens and services

1. What types of greens do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of fresh, locally-grown greens including spinach, kale, lettuce, arugula, Swiss chard, collard greens, and mustard greens. Our selection varies seasonally to ensure the freshest produce available.

2. Are your greens organic?

Yes! All our greens are grown using organic farming practices. We don't use synthetic pesticides or fertilizers, ensuring you get the healthiest and most natural produce possible.

3. How do I place an order?

You can place an order by contacting us directly through phone or WhatsApp. We're working on implementing an online ordering system for your convenience. Currently, we accept orders with a minimum of 24 hours notice.

4. How are the greens packaged?

Our greens are carefully hand-picked, washed, and packaged in eco-friendly, biodegradable bags or containers to maintain freshness while minimizing environmental impact.

5. How should I store the greens to maintain freshness?

For optimal freshness, store greens in the refrigerator in a breathable container or loosely wrapped in a damp paper towel. Most greens will stay fresh for 5-7 days when stored properly.
